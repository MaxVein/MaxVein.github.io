Become a Host-Family

What is Host-Family?

Host-Family is a program that enables a person
(or family) to become a host-family for a foreign
student who will participate in a social project
in Ukraine for 6 weeks.

Why you should be a host family?

By accepting a foreign volunteer, you are
helping him to make a positive impact on
Ukraine through the F&F socail projects
we have created to develope society.

You will gain:
  • Getting to know new
    culture at home.
  • The practice of English 24/7.
  • The opportunity to open
    Ukraine to a foreigner.
  • Exit the comfort zone and
    change your outlook.
  • A new family friend from
    another country.
